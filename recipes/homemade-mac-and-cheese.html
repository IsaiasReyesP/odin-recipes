Homemade Mac and Cheese

Macaroni and cheese, also known as mac and cheese in the United States and Canada, is a rich and creamy dish of pasta in a cheese sauce. The sauce is typically made with cheddar cheese, milk, and butter, and the pasta is often elbow macaroni. Mac and cheese can be served hot or warm as a main dish or a side, and it often has a breadcrumb topping for crunch

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter
  • flour
  • Milk
  • Cheese
  • Seasonings
  • Bread Crumbs

Steps

  1. Boil the noodles,drain,and transfer to a prepared baking dish.
  2. Make cheese sauce, pour sauce over noodles.
  3. Make topping, spread over mac and cheese.
  4. Bake mac and cheese until topping is golden brown.
