All Fruit Smoothie

A fruit smoothie consists of fruits either fresh or frozen which is then blended and served as is.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 large banana
  • 1 cup frozen strawberries
  • 1 cup frozen blueberries

Steps

  1. Pour pineapple juice into blender
  2. Add banana cut into chunks
  3. Add strawberries and blueberries
  4. Blend until smooth and serve
